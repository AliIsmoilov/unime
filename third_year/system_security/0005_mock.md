## PART 2: Short Q&A (Exam Friendly)

### Q1. What is a threat?
**A:** A potential cause of harm that can exploit a vulnerability.

---

### Q2. Give one example of confidentiality.
**A:** Only the owner can read personal email.

---

### Q3. What is authentication?
**A:** Verifying that a user is who they claim to be.

---

### Q4. What is least privilege?
**A:** Giving users only the permissions they need.

---

### Q5. What is a buffer overflow?
**A:** Writing more data than allocated memory can hold.

---

### Q6. Name one countermeasure for buffer overflow.
**A:** Input length checking.

---

### Q7. What is malware?
**A:** Software designed to cause harm.

---

### Q8. Give one example of phishing.
**A:** Fake bank email asking for login details.

---

### Q9. What is SQL Injection?
**A:** Injecting malicious SQL code via user input.

---

### Q10. What is the purpose of a security policy?
**A:** Define rules and responsibilities for security.

---

### Q11. What is business continuity planning?
**A:** Planning how business continues after a disaster.

---

### Q12. Give one example of natural disaster risk.
**A:** Flood or earthquake.
